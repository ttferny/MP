SPF mechanisms & qualifiers

ip4 / ip6

Allows a specific IP address or range to send mail.

Example: ip4:203.0.113.5
include

Trusts another organisation’s SPF rules for third-party senders.

Example: include:_spf.google.com
mx / a

Uses known mail servers or hostnames linked to the domain.

Useful for simple setups
Qualifiers

+ passes, - fails, ~ softfails, ? stays neutral.

Example: -all

Before vs after SPF

Without SPF

  • Fake emails can reach inboxes.
  • Users may trust spoofed messages.
  • Phishing risk stays high.

With SPF

  • Authorised senders are recognised.
  • Fake senders are more likely to be blocked.
  • Overall email trust improves.
