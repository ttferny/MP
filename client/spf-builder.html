How to add this to your DNS

  1. Log in to your domain registrar (e.g. GoDaddy, Namecheap, Cloudflare).
  2. Go to DNS settings for your domain.
  3. Add a new TXT record.
  4. Set the name/host to @ (represents your root domain).
  5. Paste the generated record above as the value.
  6. Save — DNS changes can take up to 48 hours to propagate.
You can only have one SPF record per domain. If one already exists, edit it rather than adding a second.
